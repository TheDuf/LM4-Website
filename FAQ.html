- FAQ -

"What is a hackathon?" "What if I have no experience?" "What should I bring?"
  • Hackathon = Invention + Marathon.

  • Our goal is to get students who have never programmed before to make something real.

  • Make something quickly in a clever way.

  • It is not about winning. It is about creating, learning, teamwork, and having fun.
  • Then you've come to the right place!

  • The Warren Hills Hackathon is open to all of our students of all levels. Beginners are encouraged to come.

  • Hackathons provide an awesome opportunity to dive into programming.

  • At this event, workshops and mentors will help you get started with the basics of programming.

  • You are guaranteed to leave our hackathon having learned a lot.
  • You should bring a laptop or desktop if possible.

  • Also bring a phone, charging cables, and any hardware you plan on using.

  • Power strips, personal hygiene products, and an extra set of clothes are also recommended.
"How much does this event cost?" "Who can attend?" "How do teams work?"
  • It's FREE!

  • Food and drink will be provided free of cost as well.

  • You will be supplied a breakfast, lunch, and dinner!

  • Any student in Warren Hills High School can attend!

  • This event is created solely for you guys!
  • You can work on a project individually or with a team.

  • You can come as a team, or form a team at the event.

  • For more information on teams, contact Mr.Detrick @ detrickd@warrenhills.org

"Can I start my hack before the event?" "How/When do I submit my Hack?"
Nope! Please don't start your hack until after the kickoff ceremony if you plan to submit it for judging. You submit your hack towards the end of the hackathon for judging - 8:00 pm.
