How was the experience?

Several Warren Hills students with all backgrounds of computer science knowledge
learned several things about programming and thinking outside of the box.
Students hacked and created applications, items that made human life simpler, and overall expanded their own limits
when it comes to their own creativity. We had an attendance of around 150 students last year and plan to increase that.
